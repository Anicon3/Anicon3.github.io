What is Anicon?

Anicon is IISER-Kolkata's premier anime and pop culture festival, bringing together fans of anime, manga, comics, and more. It's a celebration of creativity, imagination, and the vibrant community that makes these mediums so special.

Our Philosophy

We believe in creating a space where fans can come together, share their passion, and celebrate the art forms they love. Our events are designed to foster creativity, build community, and provide unforgettable experiences.
